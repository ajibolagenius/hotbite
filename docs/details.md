## Summary

Hotbite is a street-food restaurant brand presented as a full marketing website design in a single Figma file titled "🔥 Design". The visual language is bold and playful, featuring chunky orange display type, dark burnt-brown surfaces, cream pill-shaped buttons, hand-drawn flavor-tag stickers, emoji accents, and warm food photography.

### Pages

Pages are provided for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile variants.

| Page | Desktop width | Notes |

### Home Page Sections

1. Hero — "Wrapped In Flavor" headline with flavor-tag pills, pizza imagery, prev/next slider arrows, and CTAs for "View Menu" and "Find Us"
2. About — Image collage, introduction copy, and CTA
3. Featured — "Hotbite Specials" with 3 food cards
4. Slogan — "Flavor that speaks loud" statement with emoji pills
5. Menu — Categorized menu grid for Pizza, Burgers, Hot Dogs, Wraps, Wings, Rings, and Drinks, plus a promo callout for a wrap combo with fries for $15
6. Ticker — 3 scrolling marquee lines featuring delivery, freshness, ratings, and bold flavor messaging
7. Values — "Taste The Difference" with value blocks, imagery, and CTA
8. Testimonials — "Loved By Customers" with a featured story and testimonial cards
9. Gallery — "Hotbite In Action" with a horizontal image ticker and embedded promo card
10. Benefits — "Why We Hit Different" with a benefit grid and Chef's Favorite picks
11. Order — "Order Bold Street Flavor" CTA section with product imagery
12. Contact — "Let's Connect" with contact details and imagery
13. Global — Sticky menu/navigation, footer, and texture background overlay
